# Privacy Policy

**Safha (صفحة)**
**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Safha ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This policy complies with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- Email address or phone number (for authentication)
- Display name (optional)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Language and theme preferences

### 1.2 Usage Data
- Topics and interests you select
- Stories you read, save, or share
- Reading history and interaction patterns
- Streak and achievement data

### 1.3 Payment Information
- For premium subscriptions, payment is processed by Moyasar
- We store: subscription status, plan type, and transaction references
- We do NOT store: credit card numbers, CVV, or full payment details

### 1.4 Technical Data
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- IP address (for security purposes)
- Crash reports and performance data

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
Your data is stored on Supabase servers. While servers may be located outside Saudi Arabia, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by PDPL.

### Security Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits and monitoring

### Retention Period
- Account data: Retained until you delete your account
- Usage data: 2 years from collection
- Payment records: 7 years (legal requirement)
- You can request deletion at any time

## 5. Your Rights (PDPL & GDPR)

Under Saudi PDPL (Article 4) and GDPR, you have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

### Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

### Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for optional processing (like notifications).

### Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### Restriction
Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

### Complaint
Lodge a complaint with the Saudi Data & Artificial Intelligence Authority (SDAIA).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **privacy@safha.app**

We will respond within 30 days as required by law.
